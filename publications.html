Conference Paper — ACM IUI ’26

The HEART Interface: Visualizing Risk Score Uncertainty in the Cardiothoracic ICU

M. Nourani, L. Nguyen, C. Barry, Q. Jin, and M. Tory

Proceedings of the 31st International Conference on Intelligent User Interfaces (IUI ’26), ACM, pp. 1019–1036, 2026

This paper presents the HEART Interface, a visualization system designed to communicate risk score uncertainty to clinicians in the cardiothoracic ICU. The work addresses a critical challenge in clinical decision support: helping care teams interpret and act on probabilistic risk scores in high-stakes perioperative settings.
